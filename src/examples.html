Example Paragraphs

1. The Lost Hat

Jamal was playing outside when the wind whooshed by and blew his red hat off his head! He chased it across the yard, through the garden, and past the big oak tree. Just as it landed in the bushes, a friendly dog grabbed it and brought it back. Jamal smiled and said, “Thank you, hero pup!”

2. The Robot Snack Helper

In the kitchen, Lily built a tiny robot with arms that could hold a spoon. “Your job is to help me make snacks,” she told it. The robot beeped and rolled over to the fruit bowl. It picked up an apple and gently placed it on the table. Lily clapped. “You’re hired!”

3. A Rainy Day Mystery

It was raining hard, and Alex couldn’t play outside. So instead, he decided to become a detective. He found a muddy paw print on the floor and a trail of wet fur leading to the closet. When he opened the door, his cat Muffin was sitting inside with a sock on her head!

4. The Space Garden

In a spaceship floating far above Earth, Kayla planted seeds in a glass bubble. “Let’s see if flowers can grow in space,” she said. Each day, she watered them carefully. One morning, she woke up to find a tiny blue flower blooming. “We did it!” she cheered, floating up in zero gravity.

    The curious cat jumped on the windowsill to watch the birds fly by. Can you help me count how many stars are in the sky tonight? The robot blinked its eyes and said, “Let’s go on an adventure!” Please remember to share your toys and be kind to your friends. A dinosaur isn’t real today, but it sure is fun to imagine!
