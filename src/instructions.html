Get Ready to Read!

Follow these simple steps to install ReadShift on your computer.

⬇️ Download ReadShift

For Windows & macOS

1. Find the File

After downloading, look for the file named `ReadShift_Installer.zip` in your computer's "Downloads" folder.

2. Unzip & Open

Double-click the `.zip` file to open it. Inside, you will see an application named **"Install ReadShift"**. Double-click it to begin.

3. Follow the Guide

Our friendly installer will handle the rest! It automatically sets up everything you need. Just follow the on-screen prompts.

Having trouble? Visit our FAQ page for help.
