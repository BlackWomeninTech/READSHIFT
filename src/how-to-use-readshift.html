How to Use ReadShift in 3 Easy Steps

1

Enter Your Text

When your child gets stuck on a word or sentence, simply type or paste it into the text box on the main screen.

2

Read Aloud

Click the "Read Aloud" button. Our AI assistant, Nyla, will read the text in a clear, friendly voice.

3

Explore & Learn

After the text is read, use the "Define" and "Spell" buttons for extra help with tricky words. Click "Repeat" to hear it again!
