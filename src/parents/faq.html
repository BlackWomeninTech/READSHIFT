How do I renew my account?

To renew your account, please visit the main website at ReadShift.app to sign up again.

Will you sell my child's or my personal information?

No, we do not sell your personal information or your child's information. Your privacy and security are our top priority.

What is ReadShift and how can it help my child?

ReadShift is a tool designed to help parents support their children with dyslexia, reading disorders, and general reading comprehension. We provide a platform with resources and tools to make the learning process more effective and engaging.

Is there a cost for this service?

Yes. The yearly subscription for ReadShift is $39.99 USD.
