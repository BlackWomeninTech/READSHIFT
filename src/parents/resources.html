Here are some helpful resources for parents to support their children's reading journey.

Yale Center for Dyslexia & Creativity

A comprehensive resource for parents with children who have dyslexia, offering support, research, and practical advice.

Reading Rockets - For Parents

Offers tips, articles, and videos on teaching reading, with a focus on helping struggling readers and those with learning disabilities.

Understood.org

Provides personalized resources, tools, and support for parents of children with learning and attention issues like dyslexia.

LD OnLine - Reading Comprehension

An excellent resource with articles and strategies specifically for improving reading comprehension skills in children.

National Center for Learning Disabilities (NCLD)

Provides guidance and advocacy support for families dealing with learning disabilities and reading challenges.

Children's Book Council

Discover new books to engage young readers and help them find genres and authors they love, fostering a love for reading.

KidsHealth - Learning & School

Offers articles and advice on a wide range of topics related to school, learning, and study habits for kids of all ages.

Scholastic Reading Resources

Provides practical reading tips, printable activities, and fun book lists to make reading a family affair.

Understood - Dyslexia & Reading Issues

Offers specific articles and support for parents of children with dyslexia and other reading-related challenges.

PBS Parents - Reading & Language

Interactive games, activities, and articles designed to make learning to read and write fun for young children.

The Literacy Site

A website that provides free resources and supports literacy for children in need by a simple click.

The Getty - Learning Resources

Explore art and stories to build vocabulary and comprehension skills in a creative and engaging way.
