Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

Protecting the privacy of children is especially important to us. In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 without verified parental consent.

Information We May Collect

Once the product is launched, we may collect limited information from children under 13, such as:

  • A username or alias (not a real name)
  • Audio input (temporarily processed for AI learning activities, not stored)
  • General app usage activity (for improving functionality)

No personal identifiable information (PII) such as full names, addresses, contact details, or geolocation will be collected without direct parental consent.

How We Use This Information

The information we collect is used solely for educational or functional purposes within the app, such as:

  • Improving user experience and engagement
  • Providing age-appropriate learning responses
  • Supporting accessibility features (like text-to-speech)

Parental Consent

Before collecting any information from a child under 13, we will obtain verifiable parental consent through approved methods, including but not limited to:

  • Email confirmation with follow-up validation
  • Credit card transaction (no charge applied)
  • Signed consent form submitted by the parent or guardian

Parental Rights

As a parent or guardian, you have the right to:

  • Review the information we have collected from your child
  • Request that we delete that information
  • Refuse to allow any further collection or use of your child's information

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: info2@blackwomenintech.com

Data Security

We take the security of children's information seriously and implement industry-standard safeguards to protect any data we collect.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Effective Date" and notify parents as required by law.
